The Best Lemon Drizzle Cake

Why THIS lemon cake is amazing?

It strikes an ideal harmony between tangy zest and sweet depth, delivering a vibrant, refreshing flavor that feels both soothing
and refined. Its charm comes from the way various parts of the lemon are combined to create a layered taste and moist consistency.

Ingredients:

  • 225g unsalted butter, softened
  • 225g caster sugar
  • 4 eggs
  • 225g self-raising flour
  • 1 lemon zested

    • For the drizzle topping

  • 1½ lemons juiced
  • 85g caster sugar

Method:

  1. Heat the oven to 180C.
  2. Beat together the butter and sugar until creamy, then add the eggs, one at a time, while slowly mixing
  3. Sift in the self-raising flour, then add the lemon zest and mix until well combined.
  4. Line a 14 x 24cm loaf tin with parchment paper, then pour in the mixture and level the top with a spoon.
  5. Bake for 45-50 mins until a thin skewer inserted into the centre of the cake comes out clean
  6. While the cake is cooling in its tin, mix the lemon juice and caster sugar to make the drizzle.
  7. Prick the warm cake all over with a skewer, then pour over the drizzle – the juice will sink in and the sugar will form a
    lovely, crisp topping.
  8. Leave in the tin until completely cool, then remove and serve.
